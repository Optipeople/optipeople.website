### How to Add Your First TPM Alarm

1. Go to machine settings.
2. Find `TPM` in the left-side menu.
3. Press `Add` in the top right corner.
4. Give your alarm a name, description, and start time.
5. Select the type of alarm you want to create.
   Available types: Time, Production hours, Unit counts, and Telemetry.
6. Select the alarm condition based on the type.
7. Choose what email should receive the alarm.
8. Choose what phone number should receive the alarm by SMS, then press `Add`.
9. Press `Save`.

When the alarm triggers based on the conditions you set, you receive an alarm on the operator panel as well as an email or SMS.
