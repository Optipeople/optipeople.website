Carl Hansen leverages Opticloud to enhance their production processes:

Accurate Productivity Insights: Reliable data on machinery performance forms the basis for strategic decisions and analyses. Operators contribute by logging downtime reasons, providing valuable insights to target areas for productivity improvement.

Precise Cycle Time Adjustments: Operators select the specific item being produced, and cycle times are adjusted accordingly in Opticloud. This ensures highly accurate data and supports operational efficiency.
OptiAI Pilot Project: Carl Hansen is piloting OptiAI to optimize production order sequencing and reduce setup times. By suggesting the most efficient sequence, OptiAI aims to minimize transitions between orders, increasing overall productivity.
