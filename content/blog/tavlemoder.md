Opticloud is positioned here as a support tool for better production board meetings.

The core idea is simple: production data should be visible and discussable so that improvement becomes a shared process rather than a vague conversation.

## How Opticloud Supports Board Meetings

Data can be:

- printed as reports
- shown directly on a large screen
- extended with tools such as Power BI or Tableau when special reports are needed

Typical areas to follow in a board meeting include:

- uptime trends
- OEE trends
- full-production performance
- individual item numbers
- stop reasons

## Why the Meeting Matters

If the goal is to discuss trends and improvement opportunities, then production data is often most useful in a weekly rhythm rather than constant daily discussion.

The article’s main point is that teams often know where the low-hanging fruit is, but they need shared data to discuss it productively.
