Version 51 of Opticloud introduced a new graph for **units produced per hour**.

This graph aggregates output over time and makes it easy to visualize production speed at the right level of detail.

## How the Graph Works

The graph automatically adjusts based on the selected time range:

- for a single day, it shows individual hours
- for a week, it shows production by day
- for multiple months or years, it zooms out to show broader time periods

This means you can always see production speed clearly, whether you need detailed hourly visibility or a larger management overview.

## Why It Matters

The graph also includes:

- a trend line
- an average line

When you zoom out, those lines make it easier to see whether production is moving in the right direction over time.

This feature gives teams a simple way to track throughput development and spot changes in performance faster.
