OptiPeople works with machine manufacturers, automation companies, and manufacturing companies on digital IoT projects.

The approach starts by understanding the need and then defining the right technical and commercial solution together.

## For Machine Builders and Automation Companies

OptiPeople can act as an IoT partner and help develop new digital revenue streams.

Instead of building a full IoT or IIoT product from scratch, companies can use a ready-made platform and still:

- keep preferred hardware
- apply their own branding
- build services around field data

Examples of value include:

- enhanced service offerings
- stronger guarantees
- more upselling opportunities
- improved ROI cases in new sales

## For Manufacturers

If a manufacturer needs special developments, integrations, or visualizations, OptiPeople can help define and deliver the solution.

Typical project areas include:

- traceability
- digital twins
- visualizations
- PLC programming
- ERP or SCADA integration

## A Typical Process

The article describes a four-step process:

1. Understand the wishes and needs in the project.
2. Help shape the commercial proposition.
3. Define one or more technical solutions together.
4. Jointly develop and roll out the solution.

The core idea is pragmatic: start from a real business need, then design the right IoT solution around it.
