Microsoft Power BI is a strong tool for data visualization and reporting. It allows manufacturers to present collected data in the exact way they need, across both large and small screens.

## Why Power BI Matters

Many OptiPeople customers use Power BI to:

- automate reporting templates
- build the views they need
- combine production data with business data
- share key metrics across the organization
- support collaboration and compliance

Instead of downloading data from multiple systems into Excel and manually merging it, Power BI can automate that process and make reports available with little manual work.

## How It Fits with OptiCloud

Opticloud reporting supports Microsoft Power BI and includes a free reporting template.

That means customers can build on existing operational data and extend it into broader reporting and analytics workflows.

## Flexible Support Model

If a company already has internal Power BI capabilities or external BI partners, OptiPeople can support those teams as well.

The point is not to force one delivery model, but to make the data usable in the way that works best for the organization.
